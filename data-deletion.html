User Data Deletion

Last updated: June 20, 2026

SeaVera AI products let you connect third-party accounts (for example, signing in with Facebook) to verify your identity in NexusHeart. When you connect Facebook, we receive only the basic profile information you approve — typically your name, profile picture, an app-specific user ID, and (if granted) your email address. This page explains how to delete that data.

Option 1 — Disconnect inside the product

In NexusHeart, open Profile → Connected accounts and choose Disconnect next to Facebook. This removes the stored connection and the verified profile data we received from Facebook.

Option 2 — Remove the app from Facebook

In Facebook, go to Settings & Privacy → Settings → Apps and Websites, find the SeaVera / NexusHeart app, and remove it. This revokes our access. To also delete data already stored, use Option 1 or Option 3.

Option 3 — Request full deletion by email

Email privacy@seavera.ai from the address associated with your account, with the subject "Data deletion request". Include the product (NexusHeart) and, if possible, the name or email on your account so we can locate it. We will confirm and delete the associated personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain certain records by law.

Deleting your entire account

To delete your whole NexusHeart account and all associated data, use the account-deletion option inside the product, or email privacy@seavera.ai to request it.

Contact

Questions about data deletion? Email privacy@seavera.ai.
